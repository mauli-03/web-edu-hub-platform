Frequently Asked Questions

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage. Fill in your personal details, choose a strong password, and verify your email address. Once verified, you can log in and start exploring our educational resources.

Browse our course catalog and select the course you're interested in. On the course page, click the "Enroll" button. You'll be guided through the enrollment process which may include payment information if it's a paid course.

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for most courses. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can request a refund by contacting our support team within 30 days of enrollment.

Your learning progress is automatically tracked in your user dashboard. Visit your profile page to see completion percentages, quiz scores, and certificates earned for each course you're enrolled in.

Once you've successfully completed a course, your certificate will be available in your profile under the "Certificates" section. Click on the certificate to view it, then use the download button to save it as a PDF.
